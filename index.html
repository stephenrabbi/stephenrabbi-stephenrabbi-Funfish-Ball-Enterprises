Welcome To Our Farm

Funfish-Ball Enterprises is a fish cum poultry production (Integrated farming) company were we Hatch, Growout and Supplying of Live and processed Table size, Melange size Catfish, Chicken and Eggs to Nigerians at an affordable rate. In Funfish-Ball Enterprises, our major nature of business includes but not limited to the following:
1. Fingerlings production(hatchery)
2. Table and Melange Size fish production (Live and processed)
3. Production of chicken and Eggs
4. Fish, chicken and Eggs marketing/distribution
5. Online Agriculture co-investment platform.
We run an agricultural investment platform, where an investor can invest on any of our products for example, an investor might decide to invest a certain amount on our poultry bird let say broilers for two months (8 weeks) and they will get a 25% profit return on investment and investment on our layers is a long term cycle of 12 months with a 30% interest return on investment and for our catfish 5 months investment period with a return of 50% and the invested amount plus the profit is paid directly into the bank account of the investor and all is done through our digital platform with which the investor can monitor the progress of the farm and make transactions too, but 2% management fee apply across all investment types. We are fully insured by our insurance partner. Funfish-Ball Enterprises is a farm that practically deals on catfish (hatchery to grow-out), poultry birds and its product, founded with the right mindset to supply our highly esteemed clients with the best quality of fish, poultry meat and eggs. The main economic significant of the business is to contribute towards narrowing down the fish demand-supply gap deficit in Nigeria as well as the supply of proteins and micro nutrients for feeding the teeming population of Nigeria.
