F.A.Q

Funfish-Ball Enterprises is a Nigerian based Integrated Farm created to make relevant difference in the Agricultural sector of Nigeria using technology thereby creating wealth for our customers and investors. We run a complete fish cum poultry value chain agricultural business that seek to provide opportunity for our customers and local investors who are willing to invest their resources into agricultural business.

In fact, the demand for fish, chicken and eggs can never be met because of the massive population we have especially here in Nigeria. As any farm or business venture, catfish cum poultry farming involves risk. This risk if managed carefully could be minimized, as they have the potential to wipe out your fish stock, poultry birds or make the farm unprofitable.

Poultry refers to all birds of economic value to man as source of meat, egg and fibre. Poultry production is practiced in all levels ranging from subsistence to large scale commercial operations. Poultry meat and eggs are the most consumed animal protein; unrestricted by any religion or culture in Nigeria

The farm is located in fish farm estate, odogunyan to hold a capacity of 10,000 juveniles and 1,000 layers and would be fed till they grow to melange and table size and sold in the market once they are up to the desired market size. The cycle period fish is 5 months (24 – 28 weeks) and chicken 52weeks (72 weeks’ maximum). We intend to start another cycle immediately the melange and table size fishes, chicken are sold and start another cycle at the same scale, that is, with 10,000 juveniles

Demand for catfish, chicken and eggs is non-seasonal commodity, we have plans to reach out to other market in Nigeria for faster sales and better profit, we also intend to employ the service of the available modern fish driers (smoking kiln) as we have identified that dry fish and chicken yield more profit and it is preserved and sold at will. We are also planning to contact Bulk cold room buyers in our target market.

The end product of integrated fish farm is the grown up (Adult, table size or melange) fish, chicken and eggs for sale in the market. Our revenue driver, would be the sales of the melange and table size fish, chicken and eggs to the market. The revenue to be raised from this is high if we are able to work hard and ensure our fish, poultry birds are alive, healthy and well fed and reach the market desired size.
