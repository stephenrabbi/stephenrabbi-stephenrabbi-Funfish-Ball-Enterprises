Our Mission

FunFish-Bal Enterprises missions is to provide healthy fish, chicken and eggs of high nutritional value at affordable prices while improving the lives and livelihood of our customers and we also seek to provide opportunity for our customers and local investors who are willing to invest their resources into agricultural business with great ROI

Our Vision

The vision of Funfish-Ball Enterprises is to promote high proteinous food, produce quality fish, chicken and Eggs in great quantity to drastically reduce its scarcity while reducing our dependence on imported fish and chicken.

Digital Platform

We offer our investors who has the capital but do not have the land or the time to go into agricultural production or activities, an opportunity to put their money to a profitable use and monitor the progress of the farming activities that their money is been used for through our secure digital platform while we do the farming for them at a profit and our farm investors can also request a farm visits anytime, any day.

Farm Selection and Sponsor

Once an individual register on our platform by creating an account, they can select the farm units they want to sponsor raging from the production of fingerlings (Hatchery) to life and processed Catfish and Chicken to production of eggs then we use the investor funds to start up the farming, complete the full farming cycle, and cover the logistics to sell the harvest.

Farm tracking and payout

While this farm process is ongoing, the investors are able to keep track of the farm activities by getting updates in pictures and videos from their dashboard via our website or mobile app. At the end of the farming circle, the invested amount plus the profit is paid directly into our investors bank account, but 2% management fee apply across all investment types.
