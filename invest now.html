Highest Paying Agricultural Investment platform in Nigeria

We offer our investors who has the capital but do not have the land or the time to go into agricultural production or activities, an opportunity to put their money to a profitable use and monitor the progress of the farming activities that their money is been used for.

Our investors will be able to keep track of the farm activities by getting updates in pictures and videos and they can also request to go on farm visits so they can speak with the farmers and see how the farms are doing.

Funfish-ball enterprises leverage on technology to provide our investors with the highest return on investment that will be ever recorded in Nigeria through our digital platform and we also empower other small scale farmers as off takers because of our ever growing demand.

We are working to contribute towards narrowing down the fish demand-supply gap deficit in globally as well as the supply of proteins and micro nutrients for feeding the teeming population thereby reducing hunger and providing employment for the farmers and the masses.

The invested amount plus the profit is paid directly into the bank account of our investors in record time through our payment portal, because we made this our priority which have made us become the agribusiness investors' choice
